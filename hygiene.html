HYGIENE

Hygiene is the practice, disciplined way of life to live in a healthy way. Hygiene means cleanliness, harmless to one's health and germ free. We live more happily and healthily when we follow the principles of hygiene everyday in our lives. Living for a long duration is useful and enjoyable only when we live healthily.

1. WHAT SHOULD CAN DO TO FOLLOW HYGIENE?

First, should have to clean yourself daily and bathe daily.

Secondly, he should wash his clothes well in time. So, he must put on clean dress. He should put on socks shoes when he goes out of the door.

Thirdly, he should brush his teeth twice a day after breakfast and supper. Fourthly, he should drink enough clean water every day regularly. He must wash his hands well with soap before taking meals and after using the latrine.

2. What are the benefits of hygiene / the rules of cleanliness ?

It is an important part of education. It is impossible to lead a happy life by being unclean in body, mind and soul. Health is wealth. Health is the root of success happiness in life. It is hygiene which is a great source of sound health. So, everyone should follow the rules of hygiene properly.
